BIOGRAPHY

Mr. Alex Hutchins was born in Atlanta, Georgia, on August 25, 2005. He is a registered composer and publisher affiliated with Broadcast Music, Inc. (BMI) and the American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers (ASCAP). Currently, he is in his second year at Lee University, pursuing a Bachelor of Music Education. He has led ensembles in four different countries.

As a distinguished seven-year alumnus of the North Georgia School of Gospel Music, he is proud to be the first recipient of the Lilly Vee Adams Memorial Scholarship. Mr. Hutchins is actively involved in multiple community and honor ensembles in the North Georgia region and is also a former member of the Atlanta CV Drum & Bugle Corps, having marched in the summer 2023 season.

Mr. Hutchins began his conducting journey with the Liberty Middle School Chorus in eighth grade, thanks to the mentorship of Dr. Karen Graffius. He later returned to the school’s band program, where he completed a two-year internship under the guidance of Mrs. Lea Breeding as part of the Forsyth County Work-Based Learning Program.

At Lee University, Mr. Hutchins works in the Dean's office of the School of Music as one of two student coordinators. Additionally, he serves as Vice President of the Lee University Wind Ensemble.
